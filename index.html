    The way to
    Neurological Wellness

    Regional Health Survey, 2021

    Neurological disorders, including epilepsy, stroke, and dementia, affect a considerable portion of the population. In the Ashanti and Ahafo regions, an estimated 56.7% of individuals suffer from various neurological conditions

    Regional Health Authority, 2021

    Access to specialized neurological care remains inadequate. Y% of individuals with neurological disorders in these regions lack access to essential healthcare services due to geographical, financial, or knowledge barriers

    Regional Economic Study, 2022

    Neurological disorders not only impact health but also place a significant economic burden on affected families. Families spend a significant percentage of their income on healthcare and support for individuals with neurological conditions.

  • Selected work

    • Education and Awareness

      Shedding Light on Neuro Health: Raising awareness about neurological disorders and their impact on individuals and communities, driving a collective effort for change.

    • Research & Advocacy

      Innovating for Tomorrow's Wellness: Promoting and funding research initiatives to advance the field of neurological health and discover new treatments and therapies.

    • Technological Advancements

      Harnessing Innovation for Health: Embracing cutting-edge technology and digital solutions to enhance the diagnosis, treatment, and management of neurological disorders, ushering in a new era of healthcare.
